Introduction

- Urbanisation is the process of cities growing and changing, with more people moving from rural areas to live and work in cities.

Hà Nội: Sau giải phóng
★

- Hanoi, the capital of Vietnam, is an example of rapid urbanisation in recent decades.

2. What was Hanoi like in the past?

  • Before urbanisation, many areas were farmland and small villages.
  • People depended mainly on agriculture for living.
  • There were fewer roads, buildings, and modern facilities.

3. How has Hanoi changed?

- Modern apartment complexes, shopping centers, and entertainment areas were built.

- Examples: Times City, Vinhomes Smart City, Phú Mỹ Hưng (in HCM) – but you can focus on Times City as a case study in Hanoi.

- Infrastructure improved: wider roads, bridges, metro system.

- Population growth and increased businesses.
