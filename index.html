4. Positive effects of urbanisation

- Better living standards and modern housing.

- More job opportunities in services, commerce, and education.

- Improved public facilities like hospitals, schools, and parks.

5. Negative effects of urbanisation

- High housing prices and gentrification.

- Loss of farmland and traditional villages.

- Traffic congestion and pollution.

- Environmental challenges from rapid construction.

6. Conclusion

- Urbanisation has transformed Hanoi into a modern city.

- It brings both opportunities (jobs, modern lifestyle) and challenges (environment, housing costs).

- Sustainable urbanisation is essential for balancing development with quality of life.
